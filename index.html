About the Author

Anine West

Anine West is more than just an expert in child development—she's a mom of three and a proud grandmother of one, with years of hands-on experience in the ups, downs, and giggles of parenting.

She has worked closely with kindergartens, subject specialists, and child psychologists to support families with school readiness, emotional growth, and early learning strategies. Her passion is helping parents feel equipped and confident, especially in those tricky toddler years when big feelings come with small bodies.

With her blend of professional insight and real-life parenting wisdom, Anine's guides are designed to be practical, gentle, and full of encouragement—so you never feel alone on your parenting journey.
